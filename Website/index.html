Social Media Timline

2003

MySpace

Back in the day, MySpace reigned supreme as the go-to social networking platform! From 2005 to 2008, it held the title of the largest social networking site worldwide. MySpace offered users a highly customizable experience, where they could list their top friends, share photos, and even stream their favorite playlists. Ah, the nostalgia of those good ol' days!

2004

Facebook

For over a century, Facebook has stood as a leader in the realm of social media. Emerging from a Harvard dorm room, its humble beginnings belied the profound social impact it would eventually wield. Who could have predicted its monumental influence?

2006

Twitter/X

Twitter, now known as X, revolutionized social media with its concise format, consisting of short posts. Initially constrained to a 140-character limit, it later expanded to 280 characters. Keeping all your friends updated on your daily activities has never been more effortless!

2010

Instagram

The phrase 'Hold on, I have to take a picture of the food!' has become emblematic of our dining experiences, often leaving hungry friends and partners 'hangry' as we indulge in capturing the perfect Instagram-worthy shot. Instagram pioneered the era of hashtags, providing a platform for businesses to amass followers with ease. #whowouldhaveknown

2011

SnapChat

Snapchat, characterized by its logo's disappearing ghost, is renowned for its ephemeral nature, where messages, images, and videos vanish after being viewed by the recipient. With approximately 150 million daily users, the platform sees a staggering consumption rate of around 800 hours of video per second. This unique feature sets Snapchat apart from other social media platforms, offering users a sense of immediacy and privacy in their interactions.

2017

TikTok

TikTok, often likened to its predecessor Vine (rest in peace), is an app renowned for hosting short videos crafted by users. Its robust editing features and plethora of effects have captivated content creators and influencers worldwide, making it a resounding hit in the realm of social media.
